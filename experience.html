A History of Flight

1998

SkyDrift Founded

Established in Co. Kerry with a single balloon. First commercial flight over Killarney National Park.

2004

Fleet Expansion

Grew to four balloons and opened a second launch site in Co. Meath.

2011

10,000 Passengers

A landmark milestone — 10,000 passengers safely flown with zero safety incidents.

2018

Tourism Ireland Partnership

Official Fáilte Ireland approved experience, featured in Wild Atlantic Way campaigns.

2024

Fleet of 12 & New Routes

New sites in Wicklow and Clare. Launched our exclusive Sunrise Champagne Experience.
